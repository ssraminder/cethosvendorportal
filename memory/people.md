# People

Team members, stakeholders, vendors, and clients who appear in conversations or in the work. Capture enough that you don't have to re-ask who someone is or what their stake is.

## Format
For each entry: **Name** — role/relationship — relevant context (what they care about, how they prefer to be referenced, any standing requests).
